Story

The story is about the adventure of a person living in the History of Wisdom universe, where there are many beings such as Sages, Phoenix, Dragon, and Ancients.

Features

History of Wisdom is an action-adventure game that tells the fantasy medieval universe with a linear story. Combat design blends tacticality and dynamism in real-time. It offers the player an enjoyable experience with its realistic basic combat skills, magical talisman and unique story.
