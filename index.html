History of Wisdom

History of Wisdom is a fantastic medieval adventure role-playing game by Story Age.
The state of Yakukna, which had collapsed in the last decade, lost control of the land it has been ruling for three hundred years.
Yakukna state, which ended with the treaty, various bandits began to roam the territory.
The bandits are oppressing the border areas, enslaving the people in the villages.
It was time for the local people to put an end to the oppression and to establish a new state.
Some people are waiting for a hero, a legend existing in every history, but they don't know the heroes are living among them.
