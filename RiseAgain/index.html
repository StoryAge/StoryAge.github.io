Story

As a wizard, you find yourself in a mysterious land where a powerful portal has the ability to bring back the dead. However, those who pass through it return without any memories. Explore a strange new world, uncover the truth behind resurrection, and overcome its perilous challenges alongside the resurrected!

Features

Learn powerful spells to enhance your abilities in combat and exploration, and increase your attributes to become stronger as you progress. With a rich world to explore and challenging enemies to overcome, master the art of spellcasting and character upgrades to emerge victorious!
